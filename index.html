Terms and Conditions

Maximum Entry Age: The maximum entry age for Principal Members and all dependents is 84 years. Coverage cannot exceed the total allowed under each product. This policy is only available if you are over 18 years of age and not older than 84 on your next birthday.

South African Residency: The policy is only available to South African residents with a valid South African Barcoded Identity Document, and who are permanently resident in South Africa.

Important Note: Principal members are not allowed to replace deceased members. Coverage is based on the total sum insured, and adjustments are not allowed post-claim.

Insurable Interest: All insured persons must be directly related to the principal member or policy payer. The principal must have an insurable interest in each insured individual.

Waiting Periods: A 6-month waiting period applies to natural causes. A 12-month waiting period applies in the case of suicide. There is no waiting period for claims resulting from an accident.

Grace Period: A one-month grace period is granted for late premium payments. If payment is missed for two consecutive months, the policy will lapse and no benefits will be paid.

Premium Payments: Cover will only commence after receipt of the first premium. All payments must be made in South African Rand into a valid South African bank account.

Claims: Claims are paid within 48 hours after receiving all necessary documentation. No pre-existing health conditions will be penalized at claim stage.

Geographic Cover: You will only be covered for death events that occur within the borders of South Africa.

Full policy documentation will be provided upon acceptance and activation.
