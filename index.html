Terms and Conditions

Maximum Entry Age: The maximum entry age for Principal Members and all dependents is 74 years. Coverage cannot exceed the total allowed under each product.

Please Note: Principal members are not allowed to replace deceased members. Coverage is based on the total sum insured, and adjustments are not allowed post-claim.

Insurable Interest: All insured persons must be directly related to the principal member or policy payer. The principal must have an insurable interest in each insured individual.

Waiting Periods: A 6-month waiting period applies to new members for natural causes (unless stated otherwise), and a 12-month waiting period applies in the case of suicide.

Grace Period: A one-month grace period is granted for late premium payments. If payment is missed for two consecutive months, the policy will lapse and no benefits will be paid.

Full policy documentation will be provided upon acceptance and activation.
